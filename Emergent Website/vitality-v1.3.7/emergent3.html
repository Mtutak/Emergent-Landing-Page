Emergent Our Mission

We are “All In” . Not only is our design created to fit all your most important items and keep you comfortable We are committed to preserving the health and wellness of our communities by giving back. Even before our product launch we’ve organized events to give back.

Emergent was founded with one mission in mind – enabling active individuals to live a happy and healthy lifestyle. Our first product begins with a humble step in this direction - premium athletic shorts that allow athletes to securely store their essentials. We live our mission everyday by contributing to the local community and designing products that directly benefit your needs. Emergent is dedicated to designing quality activewear for the working athlete, is passionate about fostering a healthy lifestyle and lives by the mantra “Together We Are Strong”. Emergent is working with design professionals, engineers and a variety of other Chicago talent for a product line launch We encourage everyone to live their personal best in a way that inspires others to do the same. To take this philosophy into action a percentage of our proceeds will be donated to health and wellness causes throughout the community. Emergent strives to be a source of inspiration for active people everywhere.
